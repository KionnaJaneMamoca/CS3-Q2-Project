Universirty of the Philippines Diliman

The University of the Philippines Diliman is one of the country's national universities, offering a wide range of disciplines. It has a large campus and has grown significantly since its establishment. The campus is divided into four major clusters: arts and letters, social sciences and law, management and economics, and science and technology. The university focuses on academic freedom and intellectual and personal growth. It produces thousands of graduates each year and houses research and training centers.
